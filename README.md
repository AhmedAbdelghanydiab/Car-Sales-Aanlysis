# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard

## 📊 Overview

This project presents a **Car Sales Dashboard** built to analyze sales performance across different car brands, months, and years.

The dashboard provides clear insights into:

* Total number of car models in store
* Total number of sold cars
* Sales trends over time
* Performance by car brand

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Total Models Available: **66**
* Total Cars Sold: **1.35M**
* Sales show variation across different months
* Certain brands dominate overall sales contribution

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* **Monthly Analysis** → Average quantity sold per month
* **Yearly Trends** → Total sales growth over years
* **Brand Performance** → % contribution by each car make
* **Filters** → By Model, Month, and Year

---

* Power BI
* Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
